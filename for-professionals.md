I support organisations, researchers and policy teams with research-based analysis on environmental regulation, plastics policy, packaging rules and business risk.

My work can help teams understand regulatory developments, identify emerging legal, policy and market risks, compare approaches across jurisdictions, and prepare clearer materials for internal use, advocacy, strategy or decision-making.

## What I can help with

**Regulatory briefs** — clear summaries of environmental or plastics-related policy developments.

**Comparative mapping** — side-by-side comparison of approaches across countries, sectors or policy frameworks.

**Business risk research** — research-based identification of policy, market or regulatory risks linked to environmental rules.

**Background research** — evidence gathering for reports, presentations, consultations or internal materials.

**Policy materials** — support for advocacy, stakeholder communication or strategic policy work.

This work is informational and research-based. It does not constitute legal advice, compliance certification or legal representation.
