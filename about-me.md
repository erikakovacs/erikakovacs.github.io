I am a student researcher focused on environmental regulation, plastics policy, packaging rules and business risk.

My background combines legal studies, economics and analytical research. I am interested in how environmental rules move from legal texts into real-world decisions for organisations, policymakers and stakeholders.

On this site, I collect research notes, regulatory explainers and analytical projects that grow out of my work on environmental regulation and EU policy implementation.

I am not a practising lawyer. The materials on this site are informational and research-based and do not constitute legal advice, compliance certification or legal representation.
